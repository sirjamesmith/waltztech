About Waltz Technology Academic

Since 2012, Waltz Technology Academic has been at the forefront of delivering hands-on, practical training in the fields of Information Technology and Engineering. Founded with the vision to bridge the skills gap in Africa’s digital and technical workforce, we have grown into a trusted institution for aspiring professionals and organizations seeking reliable technical expertise.

Over the years, we have proudly trained and empowered hundreds of students in key disciplines including Networking (CCNA), Website Development, Cybersecurity, Desktop Publishing, Computer Engineering (CompTIA A+), Project Management, and IT Support. Our alumni have gone on to build careers in top companies, start their own businesses, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

We have also expanded our services to include office setup, electrical installations, CCTV and solar installations, supporting businesses in becoming smarter and more energy-efficient.

Our Vision Ahead

As we continue to grow, Waltz Technology Academic remains committed to:

  • Introducing more industry-relevant certifications
  • Expanding our training to online and blended learning formats
  • Building tech hubs and innovation centers across more regions
  • Partnering with global institutions to ensure our students compete internationally

We believe the future belongs to those who are prepared—and at Waltz Technology, we are preparing leaders of tomorrow, today.
